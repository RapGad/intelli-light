Frequently Asked Questions

Do these lights work on cloudy days?

Yes! Our premium solar panels are designed to capture energy even on overcast days. The high-capacity batteries store enough power for 2-3 nights of illumination.

How long do the batteries last?

The integrated lithium-ion batteries are rated for 3-5 years of daily use and are replaceable in most models.

Are they waterproof?

Absolutely. All our lights are IP65 or IP66 rated, meaning they are fully protected against dust, rain, and snow.

Do I need an electrician to install them?

No! That's the best part. Since there is no wiring, you can easily install them yourself in minutes using the included mounting hardware.
